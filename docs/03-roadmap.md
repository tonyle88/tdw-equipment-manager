# Roadmap tung phan

## Phan 1: Prototype doc du lieu mau

Ket qua mong muon:

- Web app mo truc tiep duoc bang browser.
- Co dashboard tong quan.
- Co bang danh sach thiet bi.
- Co loc theo nhom/tinh trang va tim kiem.
- Co seed data tu file Excel hien tai.

## Phan 2: Google Sheet database

Ket qua mong muon:

- Tao Google Sheet dung schema.
- Doc du lieu tu Google Sheet bang Apps Script endpoint hoac Google Sheets API.
- App co file cau hinh de gan endpoint.

## Phan 3: Cap nhat du lieu

Ket qua mong muon:

- Form them/sua thiet bi.
- Ghi nguoc Google Sheet.
- Validate bat buoc: ten thiet bi, nhom, tinh trang, nguoi su dung/phong ban.

## Phan 4: Bao tri va dieu chuyen

Ket qua mong muon:

- Ghi lich su bao tri.
- Ghi lich su dieu chuyen.
- Trang chi tiet thiet bi hien timeline.

## Phan 5: Bao cao va phan quyen

Ket qua mong muon:

- Xuat bao cao Excel/PDF.
- Dang nhap Google.
- Phan quyen theo vai tro.
- Backup va audit log.
